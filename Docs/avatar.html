Avatar

An Avatar is a visual way to represent a person or brand in the product. They can display text, icons, or images. Ace your Avatars with AceUI! Follow the guide below to make avatar of your choice.

Round Avatar

You can make round image avatar by adding class avatar-round to your avatar image.

Sqare Avatar

Just add class avatar-square to image tag to make Square avatar.

Text Avatar

Just add class avatar-square to image tag to make Square avatar.

Avatar Size

AceUI provides avatar of four size - large, medium, small, extra-small. Use class avatar-lg, avatar-md, avatar-sm and avatar-xs along with avatar-round, avatar-square or avatar-text to make avatar of respective size.
